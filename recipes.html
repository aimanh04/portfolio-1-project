Recipes

Spaghetti Bolognese

Brown ground beef with onions and garlic. Add canned tomatoes, tomato paste, italian seasoning and let it simmer for 20 minutes. Cook spaghetti until al dente, then drain. Toss with Bolognese sauce, top with Parmesan, and serve.

Chicken Sallad

Marinate chicken breasts in olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, salt, and pepper. Grill until cooked, then slice. Toss mixed greens with cherry tomatoes, cucumbers, and red onions. Top with chicken, drizzle with dressing, and serve.

Chicken Alfredo

Cook fettuccine until al dente. Sauté sliced chicken in olive oil, salt, and pepper. Make Alfredo sauce by melting butter, adding garlic, and stirring in cream and Parmesan. Combine pasta and chicken with the sauce, toss, and serve.

Veggie Omelette

Whisk eggs with salt, pepper, and milk. Sauté bell peppers, onions, and spinach. Pour eggs over vegetables and cook until set. Fold omelette in half, slide onto a plate, and serve warm.
