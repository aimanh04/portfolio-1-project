Recipes

Grilled Chicken Salad

  • Marinate chicken breasts in a mixture of olive oil, lemon juice, minced garlic, salt, and pepper for at least 30 minutes.
  • Grill the marinated chicken until fully cooked, then slice into thin strips.
  • In a large bowl, toss mixed greens with halved cherry tomatoes, sliced cucumbers, and thinly sliced red onions.
  • Place the sliced chicken on top of the salad, drizzle with your favorite dressing, and serve immediately.

Chicken Alfredo Pasta

  • Cook fettuccine pasta according to package instructions until al dente, then drain.
  • Sauté sliced chicken breasts in a pan with olive oil, salt, and pepper until fully cooked, then set aside.
  • In the same pan, prepare the Alfredo sauce by melting butter, adding minced garlic, and stirring in heavy cream and grated Parmesan cheese until smooth.
  • Combine the cooked pasta and chicken with the Alfredo sauce, toss well, and serve immediately.

Spaghetti Bolognese

  • Brown ground beef with chopped onions and minced garlic in a large skillet until the beef is fully cooked.
  • Stir in canned tomatoes, tomato paste, and Italian seasoning, then simmer the sauce for 20 minutes to develop flavors.
  • Cook spaghetti according to package instructions until al dente, then drain.
  • Toss the cooked spaghetti with the Bolognese sauce, top with grated Parmesan cheese, and serve hot.

Veggie Omelette

  • Whisk together eggs with a pinch of salt, pepper, and a splash of milk until well combined.
  • Sauté diced bell peppers, onions, and spinach in a non-stick skillet until they are tender and fragrant.
  • Pour the egg mixture over the sautéed vegetables and cook on medium heat until the eggs are set and the edges start to pull away from the pan.
  • Fold the omelette in half, slide it onto a plate, and serve warm.
