Recipes

Grilled Chicken Salad

Marinate and grill chicken breasts, then slice. Toss mixed greens with cherry tomatoes, cucumbers, and red onions. Top with chicken, drizzle with dressing, and serve.

Beef Stir-Fry

Marinate and stir-fry beef strips, then set aside. Stir-fry mixed vegetables until tender-crisp. Return beef to the pan, add sauce, mix well, and serve over rice.

Spaghetti Bolognese

Brown ground beef with onions and garlic. Add canned tomatoes, tomato paste, and seasoning; simmer. Cook spaghetti, toss with Bolognese sauce, top with Parmesan, and serve.
